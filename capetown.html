Cape Town

Cape Town is a port city on South Africa’s southwest coast, on a peninsula beneath the imposing Table Mountain. Slowly rotating cable cars climb to the mountain’s flat top, from which there are sweeping views of the city, the busy harbor and boats heading for Robben Island, the notorious prison that once held Nelson Mandela, which is now a living museum.

Tips for your first trip to Cape Town

  1. Stay close to the city centre

  2. Be weary of the expensive touristy regions (unless you’re loaded)

  3. Work with the weather, not against it

  4. The city is pretty safe, but stay alert

Things to do in Cape Town

  1. Visit Table Mountain
  2. V&A Waterfront for shopping your heart out
  3. Robben Island- Nelson Mandela was imprisoned for 27 years
  4. Penguins and swimming at Boulders Beach
  5. The Black Marlin sea-side restaurant
  6. Camps Bay for some lively night life
  7. Kirstenbosch Gardens
  8. Chapman's peak drive
