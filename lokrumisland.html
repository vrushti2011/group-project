Lokrum island is located away from the Dubrovnik Old Town (Croatia). To get there:

  • Take a boat from Porporela
  • Sea kayak
    • img source

    Why should you go? Lokrum island is a beautiful, forested island full of holm oaks, black ash, pines and olive trees. It's a popular swimming spot, although the beaches are rocky.
    The main activities to do:
  • Walk along the Botanical Gardens which was planted in the 19th centry
  • Check the main hub which is the medieval Benedictine monastery in which Game of Thrones was partly filmed at!
  • See the Fort Royal - head up to the roofs for views over the Old Town
  • Swim and snorkel along the coast of island (check FKK signs)
  • Another interesting place to swim is the 'Dead sea'

    • Some important information - Make sure to check the last boat to the mainland departs. Note that no one can stay overnight
