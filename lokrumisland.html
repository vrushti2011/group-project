Lokrum island is located away from the Dubrovnik Old Town (Croatia). To get there:

  • Take a boat from Porporela
  • Sea kayak

    • Why should you go? Lokrum island is a beautiful, forested island full of holm oaks, black ash, pines and olive trees. It's a popular swimming spot, although the beaches are rocky. The main activities to do:
  • Walk along the Botanical Gardens which was planted in the 19th centry
  • Check the main hub which is the medieval Benedictine monastery in which Game of Thrones was partly filmed at!
